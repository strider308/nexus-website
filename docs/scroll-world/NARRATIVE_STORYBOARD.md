## 3. Chapter-by-Chapter Narrative Architecture

### Chapter 1 — The Signal Appears
* **Cause**: An urgent business event / customer request (`SIG-0921`) enters the organization.
* **Persistent Object**: The **Operational Signal** (`SIG-0921`) — a glowing golden pulse node with active telemetry data.
* **Camera Movement**: Subtle forward camera push entering the spatial field.
* **Transition to Ch. 2**: The signal reaches the first organizational boundary, where legacy tools attempt to ingest it.

### Chapter 2 — The Signal Fragments
* **Cause**: The incoming signal is processed by three separate unintegrated department tools.
* **Transformation**: `SIG-0921` splits into three color-coded sub-signals:
  1. Red branch (`SIG-0921-A`): Manual spreadsheet entry.
  2. Amber branch (`SIG-0921-B`): Legacy support queue ticket.
  3. Purple branch (`SIG-0921-C`): Isolated API webhook log.
* **Camera Movement**: Camera pivots 15° to reveal three distinct depth planes separating the sub-signals.
* **Transition to Ch. 3**: As sub-signals travel further apart, their timing desynchronizes.

### Chapter 3 — The Signal Gets Lost
* **Cause**: Lack of central coordination causes the sub-signals to stall and obscure each other.
* **Transformation**: The red branch encounters a missed signature, the amber branch suffers API latency (+42m), and the purple branch logs an unlinked error.
* **Camera Movement**: Camera tilts down into a dark, occluded Z-depth angle where fragments float out of focus.
* **Transition to Ch. 4**: The **Nexus Coordinating Spine** pulses to life, sending a scanning wave across the dark space.

### Chapter 4 — Nexus Reveals the System (Turning Point)
* **Cause**: The Nexus Spine detects all three sub-signals and maps their underlying dependencies.
* **Transformation**: The three floating fragments snap into alignment along the illuminated Nexus Spine. The red, amber, and purple branches merge back into a single glowing emerald vector.
* **Camera Movement**: Camera rotates smoothly into an aligned, high-clarity perspective view.
* **Transition to Ch. 5**: The reconnected signal begins moving forward along the Nexus pipeline.
